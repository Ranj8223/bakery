Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instruction

  1. Prepare Your Pan: Line an 8x8-inch square pan with parchment paper or foil, leaving some overhang so you can easily lift the fudge out later.
  2. Melt the Chocolate: In a medium saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk, butter, and chocolate chips over low heat. Stir constantly until the mixture is smooth and the chocolate is fully melted.
  3. Add Vanilla and Nuts: Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. If youâ€™re adding nuts, mix them in now.
  4. Pour into Pan: Pour the melted fudge mixture into the prepared pan and spread it out evenly with a spatula.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate the fudge for at least 2 hours, or until it has fully set.
  6. Cut and Serve: Once set, lift the fudge out of the pan using the parchment paper and cut it into small squares.
