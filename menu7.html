ICE CREAM

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instruction

  1. Mix the Ingredients:In a large bowl, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Whisk until the sugar is fully dissolved.
  2. Churn the Ice Cream (if using an ice cream maker):If you have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into the machine and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This typically takes around 20-25 minutes, and the mixture will start to thicken as it freezes.
  3. Without an Ice Cream Maker (No-Churn Method):If you don’t have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow dish (like a 9x9-inch pan). Then, place the pan in the freezer and stir the mixture every 30 minutes to prevent ice crystals from forming. Continue stirring until it reaches a smooth, creamy consistency (this may take 3-4 hours).
  4. Freeze:Once the ice cream has thickened (either from churning or stirring in the freezer), transfer it into an airtight container and freeze it for at least 4 hours, or overnight for the best texture.
  5. Serve:After the ice cream has fully set, scoop it into bowls or cones, and enjoy your homemade vanilla ice cream!
